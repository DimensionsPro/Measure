# DimensionsPro MVP Manifesto

## Target MVP Date

**August 5, 2026**

## Statement

I am building DimensionsPro into a contractor-first measuring, estimating, and closing tool that helps window and door contractors measure accurately, quote confidently, and close jobs during the first customer meeting.

My exact goal is to create a beautiful phone/web-based application that lets a contractor capture window and door photos, use a credit card or known-size marker for scale, generate verified dimensions, select product options, calculate an estimate, and produce a clear scope of work before leaving the job site.

I understand there is no something for nothing.

In exchange for this goal, I will give consistent time, focused attention, field feedback, real contractor knowledge, and the patience to build this step by step. I will test imperfect versions, improve what breaks, and keep moving until the app becomes useful in real sales appointments.

My strict MVP target date is:

**08 / 05 / 2026**

By this date, DimensionsPro will have a working TEST web app where a contractor can create a project, capture or upload window and door photos, use a credit card or 1-inch marker for scale, receive suggested measurements, manually verify or correct those measurements, select basic product options, and generate a simple quote-ready report.

My immediate plan is clear.

First, I will make the testing branch and TEST web app stable.

Second, I will make sure saved projects reliably load from Supabase.

Third, I will make DimensionSnap work with a credit card or scale marker in the photo.

Fourth, I will require every AI measurement to be reviewed and confirmed by the contractor.

Fifth, I will add product selections, pricing rules, labor, material costs, and markup.

Sixth, I will generate a professional estimate and scope of work that can help close the customer on-site.

I will not wait until everything is perfect.

I will begin at once, test in the real world, learn from each version, and improve the app until it becomes a practical tool contractors can trust.

I see DimensionsPro helping contractors save time, reduce mistakes, look more professional, and win more jobs.

I believe this product can become real.

I believe we can build it one stable step at a time.

## Closing Line

**I measure clearly. I quote confidently. I close on-site.**
